About

Come from the planet Zarf, the Peculiars hid among the earthlings. They disguised themselves as Steves, Kevins, Stevefanies, Pandas, Owls, and even Gorillas. When their fearless leader, The Peculiar, arrived, he alone decided That it was time to reveal their presence on Earth. With the help of his Bomboys and Bomgals, the Peculiars true identities were revealed.

When you join the Peculiar Community, you have a chance at earning Passive Income. Our Loyalty Rewards Programs rewards holders for holding our NFTs in a tiered point system. 15% of all initial sales and 15% of resales is set aside for the Community, resulting in monthly payouts to each of the Loyalty Rewards members. Points are assigned to each NFT, with Peculiar Holders getting automatic entrance into the program.
